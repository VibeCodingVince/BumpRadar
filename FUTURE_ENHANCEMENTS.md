## 🚀 TO BE IMPLEMENTED

### 1. Progress Bar / Level System
**Concept:** Users advance through levels as they scan more products

- **Levels:**
  - Level 1: "Rookie Mama" (0-10 scans)
  - Level 2: "Safety Scout" (11-25 scans)
  - Level 3: "Protection Pro" (26-50 scans)
  - Level 4: "Guardian Goddess" (51-100 scans)
  - Level 5: "Wellness Warrior" (101+ scans)

- **Visual Progress Bar:** Shows progress to next level
- **Unlock Badges:** Display earned achievements
- **Profile Stats:** Total scans, safe products found, dangerous products avoided

**Implementation Complexity:** Medium
**User Value:** High - creates long-term engagement

---

### 2. Growing Bump Avatar
**Concept:** Visual representation of pregnancy journey tied to scanning activity

- **Cute Animated Belly:** Grows slightly with each scan
- **Baby Size Milestones:** Show baby size comparisons at certain scan counts
  - "Your baby is now a blueberry! 🫐" (10 scans)
  - "Your baby is now a lime! 🍋" (25 scans)
  - "Your baby is now an avocado! 🥑" (50 scans)
- **Belly Color/Glow:** Changes when scanning safe products
- **Customization:** Let users choose skin tone, outfit colors

**Implementation Complexity:** Medium-High
**User Value:** Very High - emotional connection, fun visual

---

### 3. Product Collection Gallery
**Concept:** Build a visual "Safe Shelf" of all approved products

- **Categorized Collections:**
  - Skincare
  - Haircare
  - Body Care
  - Makeup
  - Household Products

- **Visual Cards:** Show product images (if available from barcode scan)
- **Tap for Details:** View ingredients, safety analysis, scan date
- **Share Collection:** Export safe products list to share with friends
- **Search & Filter:** Find products in your collection
- **Notes:** Add personal notes to products

**Implementation Complexity:** Medium
**User Value:** Very High - builds utility beyond one-time scans

---

### 4. Daily Streak Counter
**Concept:** Encourage daily engagement with streak tracking

- **Streak Display:** "🔥 3-day safety streak!"
- **Motivational Messages:** "Keep your streak alive, mama!"
- **Streak Rewards:**
  - 7-day streak: Special badge
  - 30-day streak: Premium feature unlock
  - Freeze/pause option (1 per month)

- **Streak Calendar:** Visual calendar showing scan history
- **Push Notifications:** Gentle reminder if streak at risk

**Implementation Complexity:** Medium
**User Value:** High - drives daily active users

---

### 5. Achievement Badges System
**Concept:** Unlock collectible badges for various accomplishments

**Badge Categories:**

- **Scan Volume:**
  - "First Steps" - First scan
  - "Getting Started" - 5 scans
  - "On a Roll" - 10 scans
  - "Safety Expert" - 50 scans
  - "Century Club" - 100 scans
  - "Legendary Mama" - 500 scans

- **Product Categories:**
  - "Skincare Savvy" - Scanned 10 skincare products
  - "Clean Beauty Queen" - Scanned 10 makeup products
  - "Hair Care Hero" - Scanned 10 haircare products

- **Safety Achievements:**
  - "Clean Sweep" - 5 safe products in a row
  - "Danger Detector" - Found 10 products to avoid
  - "Wise Choices" - Avoided 20 risky ingredients

- **Special Badges:**
  - "Early Bird" - Scanned before 8am
  - "Night Owl" - Scanned after 10pm
  - "Speed Scanner" - Scanned 5 products in one session
  - "Social Butterfly" - Shared results 5 times

**Implementation Complexity:** Medium
**User Value:** High - collectible aspect, bragging rights

---

### 6. Sound Effects (Toggle-able)
**Concept:** Audio feedback for actions

- **Safe Product:** Satisfying "ding!" or chime
- **Flagged Product:** Gentle "whoosh" warning
- **Milestone:** Celebratory fanfare
- **Scan Complete:** Gentle "beep"
- **Achievement Unlocked:** Special sound

- **Settings Toggle:** Easy on/off in settings
- **Volume Control:** Adjustable volume
- **Sound Packs:** Different theme options (gentle, playful, minimal)

**Implementation Complexity:** Low
**User Value:** Medium - adds polish, optional feature

---

### 7. Shake-to-Clear / Shake-to-Scan
**Concept:** Fun mobile interaction using device sensors

- **Shake to Clear:** Shake phone to clear current scan and start fresh
- **Shake to Scan:** Shake to trigger scan button (alternative to tapping)
- **Shake for Random Fact:** Shake to see pregnancy safety tip
- **Settings Control:** Enable/disable shake features

**Implementation Complexity:** Low
**User Value:** Medium - novelty, accessibility option

### 12. Trimester-Specific Features
**Concept:** Customize experience based on pregnancy stage

- **Onboarding:** Ask "What trimester are you in?"
- **Trimester-Specific Warnings:**
  - Some ingredients safer in 2nd/3rd trimester
  - More restrictive in 1st trimester

- **Relevant Tips:**
  - "In your 2nd trimester, watch out for..."
  - Product recommendations by trimester

- **Growing Belly Avatar:** Match trimester visually

**Implementation Complexity:** Medium
**User Value:** Very High - personalization, accuracy

### 13. Barcode Scanner via Camera
**Concept:** Scan barcodes directly with camera (not just typing)

- **Native Camera Scanner:** Point and scan
- **Auto-Detect:** Automatically recognize barcode
- **Faster Workflow:** No manual typing
- **Library Integration:** Use existing barcode libraries

**Implementation Complexity:** Medium
**User Value:** High - convenience

**User Value:** Medium - convenience for some users
